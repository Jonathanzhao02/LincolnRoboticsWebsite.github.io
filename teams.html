A new team for the 2017-18 season, NT-1 had no experience, but big dreams and lot of confidence. “We’re the coolest kids on the block” says team cheerleader and engineering connoisseur Elias Raider. Although they were not as successful in competition as they hoped, in their failure they have found personal victory.
